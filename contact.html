Orders & Delivery

We offer two delivery options: home delivery and pickup locations. For home delivery, orders are delivered within 2-3 business days. Pickup locations are available at partner sites throughout the community, with next-day pickup for most orders placed before 2pm.

Delivery fees vary by location, typically ranging from $5-$8. Orders over $50 qualify for free delivery. Pickup is always free at our partner locations.

Yes! Once your order is confirmed, you'll receive tracking information via email. You can also check your order status anytime by logging into your account and visiting the "My Orders" section.

Products & Quality

Not all products are certified organic, but we clearly label those that are. All our vendors follow sustainable practices and many use organic methods even if not certified. Product descriptions include detailed information about growing/production methods.

Produce is typically harvested within 24-48 hours of delivery. Baked goods are made fresh daily. We work directly with local vendors to ensure you receive the freshest possible products, often fresher than what you'd find in grocery stores.

Customer satisfaction is our priority. If you're not happy with any product, contact us within 48 hours of delivery for a full refund or replacement. We also work with vendors to continuously improve quality.

Vendor Information

Visit our Become a Vendor page and complete the application form. Our team reviews applications within 2-3 business days. We welcome farmers, bakers, artisans, and local food producers.

We charge a small commission on each sale (10-15% depending on product category) to cover platform operations, payment processing, and marketing. There are no monthly fees or listing fees‚Äîyou only pay when you make a sale.
